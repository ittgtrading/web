About ITTG

Established capability. Safety first. Community impact.

Executive Summary

Established in 2005, ITTG Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned company founded by Mr. Itumeleng Brian Mabokela, a seasoned professional in the mining and construction sectors. We specialize in the supply, installation, and maintenance of mechanical and electrical components, supply of consumables as well as chemical and non explosive cartridge rock breaking tailored for the mining and construction industries. Our mission is to skill our employees and create sustainable employment opportunities within historically disadvantaged communities while delivering high-quality, reliable services across South Africa.

Core Value Proposition

ITTG transforms technical reliability into measurable performance and safety gains for mining clients. Our value lies in our ability to integrate field experience with engineering precision to deliver:

  • Safety and Compliance: Certified chemical and cartridge rock breaking systems that enhance underground safety, improve productivity, and pose no risk to sensitive infrastructure and machinery while meeting Mine Health and Safety Act standards.
  • Reliable Supply: Supply of all mining-related consumables, machinery, and equipment from OEMs.
  • Community-Based Delivery: Local labor sourcing and training programs that strengthen employment and supplier inclusion within mining communities.

Our Commitment to Excellence

  • Client-Focused Execution: Every project begins with understanding operational needs and ends with measurable performance results.
  • Reliable Partnerships: Strong collaboration with OEMs ensures access to proven technologies.
  • Professional Delivery: Our teams maintain high safety standards, precise documentation, and continuous support post-deployment.

ITTG Trading and Projects is not only a service provider but a trusted partner committed to advancing safe, efficient, and inclusive mining operations across South Africa.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To be a leading broad-based empowered supplier and service provider within the mining and construction sectors, with a strong focus on reliability, safety, and socio-economic development.

Vision: To set the benchmark for quality and empowerment through innovative supply and maintenance solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients and communities.
