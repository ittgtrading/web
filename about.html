About ITTG

Established capability. Safety first. Community impact.

Executive Summary

Established in 2005, ITTG Trading and Projects (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned company founded by Itumeleng Brian Mabokela, a seasoned professional in the mining and construction sectors. We specialize in the supply, installation, and maintenance of mechanical and electrical components, supply of consumables as well as chemical and non explosive cartridge rock breaking tailored for the mining and construction industries. Our mission is to create sustainable employment opportunities within historically disadvantaged communities while delivering high-quality, reliable services across South Africa.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To be a leading broad-based empowered supplier and service provider within the mining and construction sectors, with a strong focus on reliability, safety, and socio-economic development.

Vision: To set the benchmark for quality and empowerment through innovative supply and maintenance solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients and communities.
